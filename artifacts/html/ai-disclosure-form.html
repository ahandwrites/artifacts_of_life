AI Disclosure Form

This form is built on trust between you and your instructor. We know that disclosing how you used AI — or didn't — can feel uncomfortable, especially when the rules around AI are still evolving. But learning to reflect honestly on your tools and process is itself a skill worth developing.

Complete, download, and attach this form to your assignment submission.

AI tools are becoming a part of how many of us research, write, and think through ideas. This form invites you to reflect honestly on how you engaged with AI in your assignments — or why you chose not to. There's no right or wrong answer, only honest answers.

Your responses help your instructor understand your process and support a culture of academic transparency. This also gives you a moment to think critically about your own learning. Please take a few minutes to answer each question thoughtfully before submitting your work.
